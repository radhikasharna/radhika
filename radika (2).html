WEBAPPLICATION
A web application, or web app, is a software application that runs on web servers and is accessed through a web browser over the internet. Unlike traditional desktop applications, web apps don't need to be installed on a user's device and can be accessed from any device with an internet connection.
Database
A database is like a digital storage system where you can store, organize, and retrieve information in a structured and efficient manner. It's a fundamental tool used in many applications and industries to manage data effectively.
Cyber Security
Cyber security is the practice of defending computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from malicious attacks.
FOOTBALL
Football is a family of team sports that involve, to varying degrees, kicking a ball to score a goal. Unqualified, the word football normally means the form of football that is the most popular where the word is used. Sports commonly called football include association football (known as soccer in Australia, Canada, South Africa, the United States, and sometimes in Ireland and New Zealand); Australian rules football; Gaelic football; gridiron football (specifically American football, arena football, or Canadian football); International rules football; rugby league football; and rugby union football.
